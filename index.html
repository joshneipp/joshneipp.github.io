What is Career Coaching?

  • Career coaching is professional guidance and support as you discover, design, and thrive in your career.

It can be very specific, such as:

  • LinkedIn profile review
  • Resume and cover letter review
  • Interview practice to ace that interview
  • Negotiation tactics

Or, it can help when you’re just not sure what’s next. Maybe you’re wondering:

  • Should I make a career switch?
  • Which career am I best suited for?
  • Which careers are out there that I haven’t even considered?
  • How much time do I want to spend at home and how much time do I want to work?
  • Should I got back to school?
  • Should I launch my own business?
  • Can-I-pull-this-off?

Or maybe you’re feeling stuck in a rut and you don’t even know what you want to change. Maybe you’re going through the motions thinking you’re doing everything you “should,” but something still feels off.

  • What are my values and what am I uniquely suited to do?
  • How do I pursue a career change and still cover my finances in the meantime?

Or maybe you’re in the exact right place, and you just want some extra momentum.

  • What’s the best way to improve an important skill related to my role?
  • Tackle performance challenges on my team?
